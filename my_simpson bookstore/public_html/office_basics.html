Our Office Basics are bundles that contain several items needed in the classroom! Our

Numbers pack provides most of the tools needed in math courses. For this pack,

The Beginners type includes a Nifty Notebook along with a ruler, calculator and

three Practical Pencils. The Scholar type for this pack includes a custom math designed

Powerful Pad along with a custom calculator,ruler, protractor, and five Storm designed Practical Pencils

Our Journalist pack provides the tools needed for writing courses such as English and History.

The Beginners type for this includes two Nifty Notebook along with two Practical Pencils and a Precise

Pen along with a small folder. The Scholar type for this pack includes three Storm designed Powerful Pads,

4 Precise Pens and 3 Practical pencils along with a stapler, three custom folders and two packs of note cards.
